How It Works

At Estatianest, we've streamlined the property selling process to make it as fast and stress-free as possible. Our goal is to provide you with a fair offer and close on your timeline.

1

Contact Us

Reach out to us via phone or email. Provide us with some basic information about your property. There's no obligation and your information is kept confidential.

2

Property Assessment

We'll conduct a quick assessment of your property. We consider its condition, location, and market value to help us prepare different offer options.

3

Receive Your Offers

Based on the assessment, we'll present you with a no-obligation cash offer and a seller finance offer. You can review both options and choose the one that best suits your needs.

4

Contract & Deposit

Once you agree to the offer, we will send the contract, our earnest money deposit, and proof of funds for your peace of mind.

5

Close Quickly

If you accept the offer, we can close the deal quickly, often within days. We handle all the paperwork and closing costs, making the process smooth and simple.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property buying process.

How quickly can you complete a property purchase?

We can typically complete a property purchase within 7-14 days, depending on the complexity of the transaction and your specific requirements. We work efficiently to ensure a smooth and quick process.

Do I need to pay any fees to sell my property to you?

No, there are no fees for selling your property to us. We cover all the costs associated with the purchase, including legal fees and closing costs. You receive the full agreed amount.

What types of properties do you buy?

We buy all types of properties, including residential homes, apartments, commercial properties, and land. Whether your property is in good condition or needs repairs, we're interested in making an offer.

How do you determine the offer price?

We consider multiple factors when determining the offer price, including the property's location, condition, market value, and current market trends. Our goal is to provide a fair and competitive offer that reflects the true value of your property.
